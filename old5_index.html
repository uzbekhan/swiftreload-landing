About

Digital Freedom.
Community Impact.

SwiftReload Group is a fast-growing digital technology company building a global ecosystem of innovative solutions for connectivity. We serve migrants, digital nomads, and freelancers worldwide, helping them stay connected and access essential digital services anytime, anywhere.

Mission

Go beyond technology to drive ethical, inclusive innovation — allocating profits to support charitable initiatives in education, healthcare, food, and clean water access across Asia, Africa, and South America.

Vision

Shape a future where digital freedom and community impact work hand-in-hand — empowering people everywhere to stay connected and thrive.

Global Reach

Serving migrants, digital nomads, and freelancers across the world.

Innovation

Building cutting-edge digital solutions for connectivity and services.

Social Impact

Supporting education, healthcare, food, and clean water initiatives.

Inclusive Access

Helping people stay connected and access essential digital services.

Our Brands

Multi-Brand Portfolio

Operating under our multi-brand portfolio, we deliver specialized digital solutions for connectivity, travel, and intelligent automation.

SwiftReload™

B2B digital goods platform providing global mobile top-ups and gift cards through API and reseller portals.

GloEsim™

B2B eSIM distribution platform providing APIs and white-label solutions for global mobile data connectivity.

SwiftRecharge™

Global super app for digital services, enabling users to access mobile top-ups, gift cards, and everyday services worldwide.

HalloCall™

Consumer eSIM marketplace offering instant mobile data plans for travelers and global users.

SIMALLO™

Consumer platform for global travel eSIM data plans with fast activation and worldwide coverage.

eSIMvu™

Digital marketplace for travel eSIM plans providing instant mobile data connectivity worldwide.

Tareeqo™

Global learning platform connecting students with trusted educators for Quran, Islamic studies, and academic subjects through live online sessions.

AnvarAI™

AI-powered digital assistant technology designed to support automation, insights, and intelligent user experiences across platforms.

Traveeqo™

AI-powered global travel super app integrating flights, hotels, eSIM connectivity, and essential travel services into one seamless experience.
